How to Use

1. Set Your Location

Allow location access when prompted, or go to Settings > Detect My Location. You can also enter coordinates manually.

2. View Prayer Times

Home screen shows all 5 daily prayers. The next prayer is highlighted with a countdown timer. Tap the bell icon to enable notifications.

3. Find Qibla Direction

Tap Qibla tab to see the direction to Makkah. Enable device compass for real-time pointing.

4. Digital Tasbih

Use the Tasbih counter for dhikr. Select your target (33, 99, 100), choose the dhikr, and tap the circle to count.

5. Hijri Calendar

View the Islamic calendar with Gregorian dates. See upcoming Islamic events like Ramadan and Eid.

6. 99 Names of Allah

Browse all 99 Names with meanings. Tap any name to see details and mark as learned to track progress.

7. Settings

Customize calculation method (MWL, ISNA, etc.), madhab, time format, dark mode, and prayer notifications.

Tip: Add this app to your home screen for quick access and offline use!
